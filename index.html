Valencia

My home town is a lovely city. It's situated in Spain, on the Mediterranean Sea. The famous Paella was originated in Valencia, and also many other tasty dishes.




Lisbon

Lisbon is the capital of Portugal. It is a beautiful city full of history where you can enjoy good gastronomy and good wines.




Athens

The antic Athens is the perfect city to enjoy good food and get to know some of the most impressive and old monuments in the world.




Rovaniemi

The capital of Lapland offers you wide nature and the possibility of spotting northern lights. Oh! And you can meet the real Santa!
